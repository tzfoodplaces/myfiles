PILAU CUISINE.
The flavor of Tanzanian foods has left a profound impression after tasting them on ones tongue And, one will experience a fantastic feeling if you he or she gets a chance to try them. The flavor of Tanzanian food and recipes always makes people flutter and get the feeling to want to eat more and more. Despite all the recipes prepared still Tanzanian dishes taste the best mostly if prepared by the locals. There are a lot of amazing traditional Tanzanian foods you should definitely try when visiting the country, but not just that. There are also a lot of these dishes prepared internationally.


Like in many countries ,food is among the basic need required for survival and speaking of home made food ( Tanzania food),it is the best.The tasty meals made in Tanzania have not only made people to get their basic need but also food has served as a source of income and a tourist attraction to foreigners interested to learn our culture which includes the delicious meals that are prepared with uniqueness and which are nutritious.
Among all dishes served in Tanzania ,I would love to talk about the Tanzania most popular food which is the pilau nyama(spiced rice with meat).


Pilau also called pilaf(US spelling) is a rice dish which involves addition of spices with either beef,goat or chicken .Also some prefer to add vegetables like green peas ,carrots,onions and bell peppers to add flavor.This dish is cooked by spices like cumin ,cloves,cardamom and cinnamon also irish potatoes may be added,the mixture of all those when cooked to perfection it forms a nice tasty dish that will make one want to eat more and more.When cooking pilau one may prefer to use direct spices from the market or use pilau masala which is a mixture of black peppercorns, cloves,cumin, cardamom,ginger and cinnamon all in powder form.All that mixture in the masala provides a nice arouma and brown color of the pilau.

In any restaurant one will never miss pilau in the menu since it is really a popular food. It is also preferred in different ceremonies like weddings,also in festivals like Christmas periods,New year and Eid.So one can frequently have taste of this delicious Tanzanian food. The outcome of pilau mixture is very similar to the western africa dish called jollof. However,the two meals are not the same and spices used to make pilau are very different from those used to make jollof rice.
The rice used to make pilau may be the normal rice or basmat rice but in most restaurants prefer to use the basmat rice. Pilau can be served along with salad or kachumbari or vegetables also roasted sauce.
